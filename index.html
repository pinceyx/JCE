About Us

About Janta College of Education

Janta College of Education, established in 1998, has been committed to training teachers who are compassionate, innovative and equipped with modern pedagogy. Our graduates serve in schools across the region, contributing to improved learning outcomes and community development.

Our College is affiliated with CH. Ranbir Singh University JIND (H.R). College Code 1186 B.ED course recognized by N.C.T.E New Delhi.

Mission

To prepare reflective, ethical and skilled educators through rigorous training, classroom practice and community engagement.

Vision

To be a centre of excellence in teacher education, fostering lifelong learners and leaders in education.

Quick Facts

  • Affiliated to CH. Ranbir Singh University JIND (H.R)
  • Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) - 2 years
  • Course Recognition by N.C.T.E New Delhi
  • College Code 1186
  • Strong placement & practicum partnerships
  • Scholarships for meritorious & economically disadvantaged students
